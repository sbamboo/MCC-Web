About The Project

MinecraftCustomClient or mcc for short (no not Minecraft Championship), was created for my friends to be more easiely be able to download my modpacks.

The first version or V1(legacy) worked great but it was a hastle for me to publish modpacks on their because of al the prep work, thus i created v2.

V2 is very much in development but alot of the features are there, and this website exists as a simple place to download them from.

I hope it can be a usefull tool and thank you for using it!

If you have any feedback or problems contact me and ill see what i can do, sadly i can't offer support on such pre-release softwere so use it at your own risk.
